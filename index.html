What is EuRuKo?

EuRuKo (European Ruby Konferenz) is the largest and longest running annual Ruby conference in Europe, since 2003, that brings you all the good news about Ruby and its friends. Every year it takes place in a different European city, the location being chosen by participants.

G.O.A.T. EuRuKo 2024

EuRuKo 2024 will be held in Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina.

The first-ever Ruby conference in Bosnia and Herzegovina will continue the hybrid approach developed in the previous years and will be held online and in-person at the same time.

Why the goat? Read more about it here.

Location

EuRuKo 2024 will be held at Hotel Hills: Congress & Thermal Spa Resort.

This five-star resort is home to the most modern convention center in the region, has a rich thermal spa offer, it is located 2 km from the well connected Sarajevo airport, it is a 20 minutes bus or taxi ride away from the historic Sarajevo city center. Together with its nearby sister hotel, hotel Hollywood, it provides accommodation for up to 700 guests.

As guests of the conference, you will receive a discount for accommodation in hotels Hills and Hollywood and if you book accommodation at these hotels, you will get full access to their thermal spa offer, as mentioned in the form on the links below.
