Bio

José Valim is the creator of the Elixir programming language. He and his team at Dashbit help startups and enterprises adopt and run Elixir in production while contributing to the ecosystem through sustainable Open Source. He is also a Ruby Hero Award recipient and a former member of the Rails core team.
