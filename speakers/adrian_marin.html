Adrian Marin

Panel: A sustainable path in Open Source

Friendly.rb co-host and author of Avo

Panel: A sustainable path in Open Source

Adrian will join us as one of the panel participants, for the panel discussion moderated by Irina Nazarova.

Bio

Adrian is a hands-on engineer with a knack for building dev-tools and tackling new challenges. He's the creator of Avo, his first leap into entrepreneurship, where he shares the ups and downs of the journey. A fan of good company, Adrian co-founded Friendly.rb, a developer conference that brings people together. When he's not coding or learning, he's busy hosting and connecting with fellow tech enthusiasts.
