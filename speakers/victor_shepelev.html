Bio

I am Victor Shepelev aka zverok. Since March 2023, I am part of Armed Forces of Ukraine amidst Russian invasion. Besides that, I am a software architect at Hubstaff, committer of the Ruby programming language, author of several open-source projects, and writer. In tech, my main areas of interest are open data and writing lucid code. I approach the code—and the world in general—as a text that we should learn to read and write better.
