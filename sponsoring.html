What is EuRuKo?

EuRuKo (European Ruby Konferenz) is the largest and longest running annual Ruby conference in Europe that brings you all the good news about Ruby and its friends. Every year it takes place in a different European city, the location being chosen by participants.

Facts about EuRuKo

  • ~700 in-person participants expected from Europe and elsewhere (plus virtual attendees from all over the world)
  • Will be a three day, possibly multi-track conference in 2024
  • Well established in the community; it has been held since 2003 in Amsterdam, Athens, Barcelona, Berlin, Budapest, Karlsruhe, Krakow, Munich, Prague, Rotterdam, Salzburg, Sofia, Helsinki, Vilnius and Vienna
  • Some of the very best speakers from the Ruby community
  • All talks will be streamed live, recorded, and published afterwards
